Please Buy My Pens (PBMP) began as a small, and I mean a very small business where I was going broke and needed money fast. So, I came up with this side hustle of selling pens. Since then, PBMP has sold a total of 3 pens. Scroll down for more epic information that is 100% worth your time (no promises). Please do consider buying them. They're... decent quality.

Blue pens are a classic. Everybody loves them and uses them. You can go right with a trusty blue pen. This blue pen is 100% manufactured by me and not anybody else.

Black pens are also a classic. Many people prefer color though, but that’s not a necessity. The black pens work just as good.
